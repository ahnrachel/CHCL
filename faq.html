Frequently Asked Questions

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO USE WASHERS/DRYERS?

We have a variety of washers ranging in price from $2.50 for a small double-loader to $7 for a jumbo-loader that holds about 50 pounds of clothes. Dryers cost 25 cents per 4 minutes and take about 40 minutes to dry an average load at a high temperature. Please be advised that some laundry may take longer to dry (i.e. blanket and jeans…)

WHAT ARE THE BUSINESS HOURS & LAST LOADING TIME?

We are open 9-6 on Monday, Tuesday, Thurday, Friday and Saturday. We are open 8-6 on Sunday. Please note that we are closed every Wednesday. The last loading time is at 4:30.

ARE LAUNDRY SUPPLIES AVAILABLE AT THE STORE?

Yes, we have pods (soap), fabric softener, bleach and fabric sheets. However, you can bring your own detergent, if you prefer.

WHERE ARE YOU LOCATED?

We are at #5 12830 96 Avenue, Surrey, BC. We are located right next to the Shoppers Drug Mart.

WHEN CAN WE PICK UP OUR DROP-OFF?

If you drop off your laundry by 1 PM, you can pick it up on the same day. We will let you know the price and exact pick-up time when you drop it off. If you bring in your laundry later than 1 PM, we cannot guarantee that your laundry will be ready by the same day.

DO YOU DO DRY-CLEANING/ALTERATION?

No, we don’t do dry-cleaning/alteration. We only offer laundry services.

DO YOU HAVE A DELIVERY SERVICE?

Unfortunately, we do not have a delivery service at the moment. However, we will be offering the service shortly.
